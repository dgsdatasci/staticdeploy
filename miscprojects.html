Excel Spending Tracker

This project is basically just a premade excel sheet to serve as a template for one to keep track of their spending. It contains fields of the date, spending method, amount and category of spending and pulls data in from each week to give a monthly spending summary.The fields are customizable as the web application I am currently working on is an elaboration of this.
