Diabetes EDA & Predictive Modeling

This dataset was found on kaggle and includes patient variables such as age, hyptertension, heart-disease, bmi, HbA1c-level, blood glucose levels and whether or not the patient has diabetes.The dataset was reduced to about 90,000 entries after cleaning. Exploratory data analysis was perfromed with a gender focus. The dataset was then transformed to perform Linear Regression and Descision Tree predictive modeling techniques to predict if a patient has diabetes or not. The results of these models were also evaluated.

Heart Disease EDA & Predictive Modeling

This dataset was found reprocessed on kaggle from Cleveland UCI and includes variables of patient test results such as age, sex, cholesterol levels, resting blood pressure, fasting blood sugar levels and more. Exploratory data analyst was performed through comparison of these variables. Minimal cleaning of this dataset was required as this dataset was transformed to perform Linear Regression, Deision Tree and Random Forest predictive modeling to determine if a patient has heart disease. The results of these models were also evaluated.
